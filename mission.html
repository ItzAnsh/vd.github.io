Our Mission & Vision

“Empowering students with dreams” is our tagline and our mission. We help turn your wildest ideas, and your craziest daydreams into working systems all while helping you to maintain a calm state of mind. We look to empower students all around the world.

“To the top” is the idealogy followed here at VD and we wish to inculcate our 3 pillars, empowering students, spreading awareness about entrepreneurship, and helping with mental well-being into the curriculums of students in such a way that it doesn’t interfere with their schedule but still helping them go to the top.

About Us

Incubated at Chitkara Innovation Incubator Foundation (CIIF) as an educational startup. Victory Dome’s founder Manik Chopra had one vision, to provide personalized learning eco-system to the student community.

We teach our kids social science but forget about their social skills, we teach them to learn but don’t encourage them to think, we teach them about mental health but never enquire about their own, this is why VD exists, to empower students, to help them think and discover entrepreneurship and for them to have a flourishing state of mind.
