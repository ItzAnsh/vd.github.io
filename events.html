Victory Talks

Ever felt so deeply about a topic that you need to talk about it in front of the world? We bet you have, but we also know that the world is not all that ears in to listen to a school students talk about matter they think are important. Hence, Victory Talks, we want you to “share to inspire”. Inspire not only your peers but the world, even your parents. Victory Talks is an event held by VD wherein top thinkers of top schools in the tricity come forward to speak on topics they feel the need to address in front of an audience that ranges from school students to college scholars, from professors to celebrities and your parents too. We look to make a new generation of thinkers that are also doers. Join VD and let’s go “to the top”.

Hack Your Brain

The human mind is a complex place where it is bound that a good idea will strike more often than not. But the human mind is also a trickster which is quite capable of convincing yourself that the said idea won’t succeed, but how will you know if you don’t try? Beat the trickster, join the doer. Become a young unicorn at Victory Dome and join our 2-day-long event with an 8-hour track, where from ideation to pitching we will tell you everything about entrepreneurship. Also a little spoiler, at Young Unicorns you will be at a high risk of contagious laughing, team building, and racking your brains. So join us in our mission of “creating a wave of young entrepreneurs”.
